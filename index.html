What I Love

I am in to all types of things espcially things related to computers. gaming mainly on the computer, performance and aftermarket things on vehicles, AWD cars especially Subuarus' and of course music.

Hobbies

My hobbies are really related to the things I love. coding, installing things onto my car, gaming, and jailbreaking. I got into all of these hobbies throughtout the course of my life. Time flies by fast.

Goals To Reach

My Planned Out Future.

In life, all of the people on this living Earth has goals as well as some achievements they would like to accomplish! Well, here are some goals I plan to reach before the human kind reaches to the end or if Donald Trump gets elected for president.

Build Custom Computers

I fell in love with the computer society. I love how fascinating computers can run with a single or multi core render. Therefore my goal is to create a beast out of a computer with the right parts so I can run the high end games, edit Youtube videos on the go, and pretty much multitask everything without my pc slowing down like a bucket.

Retrieve A College Degree

After highschool, I'd like to go to college. The college I am interested in is MTI! College of Sacramento. The reason why I picked this college is because MTI is very close to my other house. Makes commutes for my friend Ismael and I much much easier. Plus rent is free!

Customize Icey

Ever since I watched the movie "Born To Race", I've always wanted a Subaru. Specificly a 2004 Subaru Impreza WRX STi. Later in the years of begging for a new car, I have finally been fulfilled with the hopes and dreams by getting Icey! A 2015 Subaru WRX in Ice Metallic Silver. I plan to create Icey the way I would like her to be. Performance parts and a few exterior parts will do. Stage 2 sounds nice. AWD FOR THE WIN!

Create Codes For Big Companies.

According to my "Hobbies" and "What I Love" on my about me section, I really love to code. coding is a powerful thing especially because of now security issues in the technology field is getting into deep stuff. Lots of people try to crack, hack, and exploit lots of products of big companies. Well, the company i would love to work at is GoPro, Apple, Razer Inc., Subaru, and maybe the Navy making codes. Codes es fuego! (it means fire in Spanish)

Make Lots Of Money!

As everyone knows. Everyone loves money no matter what you say, you will always love money. money can buy you everything. Sometimes happiness but for sure cannot buy love. Love is something special. I wanna make lots of money for my family and Icey so I can put alot of things on her. I'll plan to pay off my parents bills when I get up there.

Pay off Parents Bills!

One day I will repay my parents by paying off every single cent of they're bills off so that they can live a much happier life. Money will obviously help out this equation. That's why money can buy happiness because it can make stress go away and help you catch a breath.
